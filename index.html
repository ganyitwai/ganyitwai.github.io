Malaysia background:
Malaysia has a tropical rainforest climate and is located near the equator, so it is hot throughout the year. It is surrounded mostly by sea and contains many islands, some of which are Betruit Island, Langkawi, and Penang Island. Half of Malaysia is covered in trees, hence the climate. Some of these forests have become preserved and turned into parks for visitors. 

China background:
China has tropical monsoon climate zone, subtropical monsoon climate zone, temperate monsoon climate zone. Chinese culture has a long history, extensive and profound, and is colorful. As the cultural supremative country of the East Asian cultural circle, Chinese culture occupies an important position in the world cultural system. Due to the differences in geographical location and natural conditions, Chinese culture and economy also have their own characteristics.
